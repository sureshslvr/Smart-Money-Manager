# 💰 Smart Money Manager - Free AppSheet Template

## Why This App Exists

Most expense tracking solutions focus on subscriptions, premium features, advertisements, or storing your financial data on third-party systems.

This app was built with a different goal:

**A completely free personal finance management solution where your data remains under your control.**

All your financial records are stored in your own Google account and can be accessed, exported, or backed up by you at any time.

The purpose of this app is simple:

> Help individuals and families understand exactly where their money is going and build better saving habits through consistent expense tracking.

Many people know their monthly income but struggle to identify where their money disappears. The only reliable way to improve savings is to track every income and expense, no matter how small.

By recording every transaction, you gain visibility into:

* Spending patterns
* Unnecessary expenses
* Monthly savings potential
* Financial goals progress
* Income vs Expense trends

Even small daily expenses can add up significantly over time. This app helps make those expenses visible.

# Adding Income

To add a new income entry:

1. Open the Income section
2. Click the **+ (Add)** button
3. Enter income details
4. Click **Save**
![Add button](https://github.com/sureshslvr/Smart-Money-Manager/blob/efd695d64a2a098b950668331e1eeb0204db55fa/screenshots/Screenshot_20260620-190452.jpg)
Examples:

* Salary
* Bonus
* Freelancing
* Business Income
* Other Earnings

---

# Adding Expenses

To add a new expense:

1. Open **My Expenses**
2. Click the **+ (Add)** button
3. Enter expense details
4. Select category
5. Click **Save**
![Add button](https://github.com/sureshslvr/Smart-Money-Manager/blob/efd695d64a2a098b950668331e1eeb0204db55fa/screenshots/Screenshot_20260620-190452.jpg)
For best results, record expenses immediately after spending.

Examples:

* Groceries
* Fuel
* Rent
* Shopping
* Utilities
* Entertainment

# Restoring Deleted Entries

Deleted entries can be restored.

Steps:

1. Open the navigation menu (☰)
2. Select **Deleted Expenses**
3. Select the record(s) to restore
4. Click **Undo**

The entry will be restored to active records.

![deleted entry](https://github.com/sureshslvr/Smart-Money-Manager/blob/efd695d64a2a098b950668331e1eeb0204db55fa/screenshots/Screenshot_20260620-190633.jpg)
